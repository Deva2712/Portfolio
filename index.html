OUR PROJECTS

Nivara

Jabalpur

3 months

Nivara is imagined as a quiet retreat where warmth, balance, and modern simplicity come together to create a sense of belonging. Wrapped in soft neutrals and gentle light, the space feels calm from the very first moment. Fluted wall textures, muted fabrics, and clean-lined forms build a contemporary rhythm, while warm wood accents and handcrafted details introduce an Indian touch rooted in familiarity and comfort. Sculptural lighting and thoughtful detailing add depth without overwhelming the eye. The mood is restful yet refined a place that slows your breath, softens the noise of the day, and allows you to feel grounded, held, and completely at home.

Team Pixel's Head Quarter

Jabalpur

1 month

Team Pixel is conceived as a living canvas a studio where ideas sharpen into images and stories find their frame. Crisp whites and charcoal tones create clarity and focus, while deep teal walls and bold crimson accents introduce drama, contrast, and visual energy reminiscent of a striking photograph. Glass partitions invite light and transparency, and curated frames transform memories into daily inspiration. Sculptural lighting, reflective surfaces, and modern lines establish precision, while greenery softens the atmosphere with calm. Working here feels purposeful and invigorating a space that sharpens vision, fuels imagination, and inspires creators to see deeper, think bolder, and craft stories that endure.

Pavilion

Jabalpur

25 days

Studio Y's home unfolds in calm neutrals and soft textures, where every detail whispers intention. Sculpted cabinetry with fluid grooves, matte finishes, and floating glass shelves create a refined visual rhythm. A plush beige sofa and textured upholstery soften the geometry, while glass partitions frame the principal designer's cabin, allowing light, transparency, and quiet authority to coexist. Minimal frames, curated objects, and ceramic vases add personality without clutter, while living greens breathe warmth into the space. Clean lines, warm lighting, and muted tones build focus and serenity. Working here feels composed and elevating a place that inspires clarity, creativity, and design that is truly aesthetic, not ordinary.

Katni

2 months

This commercial workspace is designed for momentum a linear, light-filled environment where productivity flows without friction. Clean white surfaces and warm wood shelving create visual balance, while matte black metal frames and exposed track lighting introduce structure and rhythm. Modular workstations line the space with precision, softened by planter greens that bring calm focus. Curated art panels and sculptural décor add personality without distraction, while glass partitions maintain openness and transparency. Thoughtful storage, layered lighting, and refined textures support efficiency and clarity. Working here feels energized yet composed a place where concentration sharpens, ideas move swiftly, and focus runs at full speed.

How We Do It?
THE PROCESS

#01.

Tech Specification

Defining the technical foundation of the project measurements, layouts, requirements, and functional details to ensure precision and feasibility.

#02.

Concept Sketch

Translating ideas into initial visual forms through rough sketches that capture the essence, mood, and direction of the design.

#03.

Design Project

Developing the chosen concept into a cohesive design refining layouts, detailing elements, and aligning aesthetics with functionality.

#04.

3D Visualization

Bringing the design to life through realistic 3D renders, allowing a clear understanding of space, materials, and overall experience.

#05.

Estimation

Providing a detailed cost breakdown, ensuring transparency and alignment with the project budget before execution begins.

#06.

Materials, etc

Curating and finalizing materials, finishes, and textures that define the look, feel, and durability of the design.

#07.

Supervision

Overseeing the execution process to ensure every detail is implemented accurately and meets the intended design standards.
